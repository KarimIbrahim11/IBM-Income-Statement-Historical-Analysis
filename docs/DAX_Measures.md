# IBM Dashboard - DAX Measures Documentation

This document provides a comprehensive technical and functional catalog of the DAX measures used in the IBM Financial Performance Dashboard.

---

## 📊 Core Financial Measures & KPIs

| Measure | DAX Formula | Business Purpose / Insight |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Revenue** | `SUM([totalRevenue])` | Calculates the gross top-line revenue generated by the business over the selected period. |
| **Total Gross Profit** | `SUM(Table2[grossProfit])` | Computes the absolute profit remaining after subtracting the direct costs of goods sold or services delivered (COGS). |
| **Gross Margin** | `DIVIDE([Total Gross Profit],[Total Revenue])*100` | Evaluates pricing power and production efficiency, showing the percentage of revenue retained before operating overhead. |
| **Operating Margin** | `DIVIDE(SUM([operatingIncome]),[Total Revenue])*100` | Measures operational profitability by showing the percentage of revenue left after covering all variable and fixed operating costs. |
| **Net Margin** | `DIVIDE(SUM(Table2[netIncome]),[Total Revenue])*100` | The ultimate bottom-line efficiency metric; shows exactly how much of each dollar earned translates into take-home net profit. |
| **EBITDA Margin** | `DIVIDE(SUM(Table2[ebitda]),[Total Revenue])*100` | Assesses core cash-generation efficiency, stripping out the distortions of capital structure, non-cash expenses (D&A), and tax jurisdictions. |
| **Expense Ratio** | `DIVIDE(SUM(Table2[operatingExpenses]),[Total Revenue])*100` | Tracks structural overhead efficiency by determining what percentage of top-line revenue is consumed by total operating expenses. |
| **SG&A Ratio** | `DIVIDE(SUM([sellingGeneralAndAdministrative]),[Total Revenue])*100` | Monitors commercial and administrative leanest by tracking the proportion of revenue spent on corporate overhead and sales. |
| **R&D Ratio** | `DIVIDE(SUM(Table2[researchAndDevelopment]),[Total Revenue])*100` | Measures innovation reinvestment intensity, indicating how aggressively the company is funding future technology pipelines. |
| **Tax Rate** | `DIVIDE(SUM(Table2[incomeTaxExpense]),SUM(Table2[incomeBeforeTax]))*100` | Calculates the effective corporate tax rate by evaluating total tax obligations against pre-tax earnings. |
| **Interest Coverage** | `DIVIDE(SUM(Table2[ebit]),SUM(Table2[interestExpense]))` | A crucial solvency metric that measures financial risk by showing how many times over operating profit (EBIT) can cover interest obligations. |
| **Revenue LY** | `CALCULATE([Total Revenue],SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR(Table2[date]))` | A time-intelligence measure that pulls the previous year's revenue baseline to allow for real-time Year-over-Year (YoY) variance analysis. |
